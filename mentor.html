About Ms. Ajetrao Ma'am

Ms. Ajetrao Ma'am is the founder and lead teacher of Vidyatam Classes. She is deeply passionate about helping young students develop strong academic foundations and a love for learning.

With a student-centered teaching approach, she focuses on concept clarity, disciplined study habits, and regular practice to ensure every child understands the subject thoroughly.

Her dedication to each student's progress creates a supportive environment where children feel confident to ask questions and improve their academic performance.

Teaching Philosophy

Concept Clarity

Every topic is explained in a simple and understandable way so students build strong fundamentals.

Personal Attention

Each student receives individual guidance to ensure they understand the subject properly.

Regular Practice

Frequent tests and exercises help students strengthen their knowledge and improve performance.

Friendly Environment

A supportive and encouraging environment where students feel comfortable learning and growing.
